BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a calculation that estimates a person’s body fat based on their weight and height. It helps determine whether a person is underweight, normal, overweight, or obese and gives an idea of overall health. Maintaining a healthy BMI reduces the risk of diseases and improves overall well-being.

BMI Information

BMI measures body fat based on height and weight. Regular monitoring can guide better lifestyle.

  • Underweight: Less than 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: 30 or more
